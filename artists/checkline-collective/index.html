Checkline Collective

SkaRocksteady
Denver, Colorado

About

Checkline Collective is an eight-piece ska and rocksteady outfit based in Denver, Colorado. Formed in 2018, the band has built a devoted following across the Front Range through relentless gigging and a commitment to the source material — original Jamaican ska and rocksteady of the 1960s, refracted through 2 Tone and their own contemporary sensibility.

Their horn arrangements draw directly from Skatalites-era recordings while their rhythm section pushes toward a tighter, more modern pocket. The result is a live show that works as much for longtime ska devotees as for first-time listeners.

The band's 2023 EP Offbeat City received coverage across ska blogs and college radio stations. They are currently working on their debut full-length.
