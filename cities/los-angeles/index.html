About the Scene

Los Angeles invented the third wave. Long Beach, Orange County, the South Bay — this region produced the records that defined ska-punk for a generation. The scene never stopped. The scene here is built on a small number of committed venues and bands who keep the calendar alive year-round. If you're visiting or new to the city, these are the shows and spaces worth knowing.
