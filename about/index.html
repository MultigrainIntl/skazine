Our Story

Independent Ska Editorial.
Built for the Scene.

SKAZINE exists because ska deserves a home that treats it with the same editorial seriousness as any other genre. Not a footnote. Not a niche curiosity. A full-blown music platform built for fans, artists, and scene-builders.

Mission

What We're
Building

SKAZINE is an independent music editorial platform dedicated to ska and its related genres — rocksteady, 2-Tone, ska-punk, and everything in between. We write about artists because they deserve to be written about, not because an algorithm told us to.

We cover the full spectrum: the Jamaican originators of the 1960s, the British 2-Tone explosion of '79, the American third-wave ska-punk era, and the vibrant scene that exists today — often invisible to mainstream music press.

Every artist profile, show listing, and feature article is written by people who love this music. No content farms. No SEO-bait listicles. Just genuine coverage for a genre that deserves it.

What We Stand For

Our Editorial Values

Independence

No corporate ownership. No label deals that influence coverage. We write what we believe, not what we're paid to believe. Our editorial voice is our own.

Accuracy

We verify dates, lineup details, and historical claims. Genre history matters. We don't conflate rocksteady with ska, or let sloppy attribution slide. The record matters.

Community

SKAZINE is for the scene, not just about it. We feature independent artists, list local shows, and give platforms to people doing the work — promoters, labels, venues, zines.
