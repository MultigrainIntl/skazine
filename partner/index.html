Our Audience

Who Reads SKAZINE

SKAZINE readers are passionate music fans who attend live shows regularly, buy records, support independent artists, and seek out new sounds within the ska ecosystem — from original Jamaican ska to 2-Tone to ska-punk.

Our audience skews 22–45, split between long-time ska veterans and new fans discovering the genre through modern acts. They're active on social media, frequent record stores, and travel for shows.
